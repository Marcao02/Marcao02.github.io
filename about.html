Marcos Da Silveira is a Leader Researcher at the Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology (LIST), HUMOD group. Dr. Da Silveira’s research interests focus on making knowledge-based systems more collaborative and understandable. He's an expert in developing hybrid AI approaches that bring together traditional reasoning with modern statistical methods, helping the development of intelligent software agents and decision support systems. Dr. Da Silveira’s work, rooted in Semantic Web Technologies, Machine Learning, Generative AI, and Formal Reasoning, has been instrumental in projects that ensure extracting of key information from multimodal sources, keeping the semantics of knowledge graphs accurate and up-to-date over time, transform complex guidelines into clear and formal representations, and finally bridge the gap between personal competences, industry needs, and existing trainings. His work was key to promote secure ways to share medical data in Luxembourg – notably as a designer of the architecture of Luxembourgish eHealth Platform, implemented by the eSanté Agency. Perhaps one of his most significant contributions is being a lead designer and developer of Dynaccurate, an innovative technology that led to the creation of a successful Luxembourgish spinoff company! With a Ph.D. in Industrial Informatics from University of Toulouse (France, 2003) and postdoctoral research at LAAS - CNRS, Dr. Da Silveira brings a wealth of experience from his academic roles in Brazil (Pontifical Catholic University of Parana) and Luxembourg (University of Luxembourg) to his research work at LIST.
