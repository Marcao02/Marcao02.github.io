About

Leading Research in Knowledge-Based Systems and AI

Dr. Marcos Da Silveira is a Leader Researcher at the Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology (LIST), specializing in making knowledge-based systems more collaborative and understandable. With expertise in developing hybrid AI approaches that combine traditional reasoning with modern statistical methods, he helps advance intelligent software agents and decision support systems.

His work spans Semantic Web Technologies, Machine Learning, Generative AI, and Formal Reasoning, contributing to projects that extract key information from multimodal sources, maintain knowledge graph accuracy over time, and bridge the gap between industry needs and educational training.
